inFAMOUS: Second Son — Karma

🔊

The lightbar tracks Delsin's Karma live: neutral white at the start, sliding to blue for Good or red for Evil as you play.

Documented behavior: the lightbar shifts from neutral white toward deep blue (Good) or red (Evil) as your Karma meter fills.

Spyro Reignited Trilogy — Sparx Health

🔊

Sparx the dragonfly is Spyro's health meter, and the lightbar mirrors his color on PS4.

Documented behavior: Sparx (and the lightbar) goes gold → blue → green, then red once Sparx disappears and Spyro is one hit from losing a life.

Killzone: Shadow Fall — Health

🔊

One of the very first DS4 lightbar demos — Sony's Shuhei Yoshida showed it live: green fades to red as you take damage, and back to green when healed.

Documented behavior: Sony's own worldwide studios chief demonstrated this live in 2013 — health drops shift the lightbar smoothly from green through orange to red, reversing as you heal.

Tomb Raider (Definitive Edition) — Torch

🔊

A fan-favorite detail from launch — the lightbar flickers like real firelight whenever Lara's torch is lit.

Documented behavior, widely praised at launch: the lightbar mimics flickering firelight to match the torch on screen.
